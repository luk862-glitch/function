# Spark 🌟

Whether you're meeting new coworkers, going on a first date, or just want a question to sit with on your own — good conversations and reflections don't always come naturally. Spark is a reflection and conversation starter generator that gives you a prompt tailored to your vibe, the social setting you're in, and what you want to talk about — so you always have questions that help you get to know yourself and others more.

---

## How it works

Spark is a three-page experience:

1. **Landing** — A quick intro and a "Let's get started" button
2. **Pick your settings** — Choose a mode, setting, and topic
3. **Get your prompt** — A randomly generated question based on your selections. Not feeling it? Hit Next. Love it? Save it.

---

## Features

- Random prompt generation based on mode, setting, and topic
- Save your favourite prompts and view them in a modal
- Back button to tweak your input selections
- Next button to get another ramdomly generated question based on user's selection

## Selections

**Mode**
- Playful
- Reflective
- Intimate

**Setting**
- Solo
- Duo
- Small Group (3-5)
- Large Group (6+)

Note: "Solo" option is for self-reflection — journaling prompts, thinking through your own answers before bringing a topic up with someone else, or honestly just for curiosity — sometimes you want a good question to sit with on your own.

- Work
